How to use Brutask for your team

Siddhita Upare, Founder - Brucira

  • 1 Start by asking your team members to add their daily tasks on Brutask.
  • 2 Then, the manager can take a quick stand-up to understand everyone's priorities for the day.
  • 3 Team members need to check off their completed tasks as they finish.
  • 4 At the end of the day, a wrap-up call will help you understand the day's progress.
  • 5 The completed tasks disappear leaving a clean slate. Uncompleted tasks remain highlighted in red to draw attention.
